<h1 class="post-title">🖐🏽 Privacy Policy</h1>

<p class="post-deck">The below policy applies to all information collected by Journal Mapper on the iOS platform.</p>

<h2 class="page-heading">Information Collected</h2>

<p class="role-post-body">Journal Mapper does not 🙅🏾 collect any personal information from its users. There are no ads (targeted or otherwise) or third party links within the app.</p>

<h2 class="page-heading">Location Services</h2>

<p class="role-post-body">Location services are requested within the app primarily to display the user's current location 📍 on the map 🗺. In addition, it is also utilised to provide more relevant search 🔍 results when performing a location search. While both these features enhance the user experience of the application, they are not critical to its functionality and can be switched off by the user.</p>

<h2 class="page-heading">User Data</h2>

<p class="role-post-body">The user's device 📱 is the primary location for any data the user enters. iCloud service ☁️ is used to mirror this data to enable sync 🔄 features between all devices signed into your Apple ID.</p>

<h2 class="page-heading">User Consent</h2>

<p class="role-post-body">By using Journal Mapper, you consent to this privacy policy.</p>

<h2 class="page-heading">Contact</h2>

<p class="role-post-body">If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please find the relevant contact information at the bottom of this page.</p>
